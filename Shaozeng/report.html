Introduction

With the development of the science, technology field and the computer field, human life and work was be reformed and improved.

Robot is the common name of automatic control machine (Robot). Automatic control machine includes all machinery that simulates human behavior or thought and simulates other creatures (such as robot dog, cat or even human!). In the narrow sense, there are still many classifications and disputes about the definition of robots. Some computer programs are even called robots.

This essay will discuss in detail many aspects of the robotics field, such as Android robots, unmanned robots, self-driving car robots and its future development

The meaning of the creation of a robot is an artificial machine device that can automatically perform tasks to replace or assist humans in their work.
