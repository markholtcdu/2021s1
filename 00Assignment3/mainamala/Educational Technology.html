Educational Technology

Educational technology is a field of study that investigates the process of analyzing, designing, developing, implementing and evaluating the instructional environment and learning materials in order to improve teaching and learning .Nowadays the learnings materials have gently expanded because of various technological advances include more conventional materials, such as the televisions, VCRs, slide projectors, blackboard, overhead projectors, televisions, and opaque projectors, LCD projectors, as well as newer materials , such as various software applications, audio and video conferencing and so on.And teachers should know the information about the materials which will be useful for the students or about how to use those instruments, why they should be use, when they should be use and how to integrate them into the teaching /learning environment in order to meet the ultimate goal of improving education .According to Richey, R.C.(2008) educational technology is defined as the study and ethical practice of facilitating learning and improving performance by creating, using and managing appropriate technological processes and resources
