Self Driving Cars

Self Driving Cars (Autonomous vehicles), also known as driverless cars, computer-driven cars, or wheeled mobile robots, are smart cars that realize driverless driving through a computer system.

In the 20th century, there have been decades of history, and the beginning of the 21st century showed a trend close to practicality.

Self-driving cars rely on artificial intelligence, visual computing, radar, monitoring devices, and global positioning systems to work together to allow computers to automatically and safely operate motor vehicles without any human active operation.
